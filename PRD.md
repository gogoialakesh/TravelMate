# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# TravelMate

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

---

# 1. Product Vision

TravelMate is a collaborative travel planning platform that helps travelers organize trips together, share responsibilities, coordinate resources, split group costs, and preserve trip memories.

The platform is not a travel agency and does not sell travel packages. Instead, it provides tools that enable travelers to plan and manage trips collaboratively.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Travelers often face challenges when organizing group trips:

- Trip planning is scattered across messaging apps.
- Responsibilities are not clearly assigned.
- Shared equipment management is difficult.
- Group expenses become confusing.
- Travel memories are spread across multiple devices.
- Solo travelers struggle to find compatible travel companions.

Current solutions focus on booking services rather than collaborative trip management.

TravelMate addresses these challenges through a centralized travel collaboration platform.
